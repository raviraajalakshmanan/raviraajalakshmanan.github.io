## Currently Reading

- **[Psycho-Cybernetics](https://www.amazon.com/Psycho-Cybernetics-Maxwell-Maltz/dp/0671700758)** — Maxwell Maltz · On reprogramming self-image as the foundation of performance and behavior change.
- **[Patterns of Distributed Systems](https://www.amazon.com/Patterns-Distributed-Systems-Unmesh-Joshi/dp/0138221987)** — Unmesh Joshi · A catalog of patterns — write-ahead log, leader election, heartbeat — that recur across distributed databases and message brokers.
- **[Designing Data-Intensive Applications](https://www.amazon.com/Designing-Data-Intensive-Applications-Reliable-Maintainable/dp/1449373321)** — Martin Kleppmann · The definitive guide to building reliable, scalable, and maintainable data systems.

## Engineering & Systems

### Design Patterns

| Book                                                                                                                                                       | Author                     | Notes                                                                                                 |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ★ [Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software](https://www.amazon.com/Design-Patterns-Elements-Reusable-Object-Oriented/dp/0201633612) | Gang of Four               | The canonical reference. Creational, structural, behavioral — still the vocabulary the industry uses. |
| ★ [Head First Design Patterns](https://www.amazon.com/Head-First-Design-Patterns-Brain-Friendly/dp/0596007124)                                             | Freeman & Robson           | Best way to actually internalize patterns — visual, fun, rigorous enough.                             |
| [Head First Object-Oriented Analysis and Design](https://www.amazon.com/Head-First-Object-Oriented-Analysis-Design/dp/0596008678)                          | McLaughlin, Pollice & West | Grounds OOP principles in real problems before moving to patterns.                                    |

### System Design & Architecture

| Book                                                                                                                                        | Author           | Notes                                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ★ [Designing Data-Intensive Applications](https://www.amazon.com/Designing-Data-Intensive-Applications-Reliable-Maintainable/dp/1449373321) | Martin Kleppmann | Best systems book I've read. Required for anyone building at scale.                                                                                                 |
| ★ [Software Architecture: The Hard Parts](https://www.amazon.com/Software-Architecture-Hard-Parts-Tradeoff/dp/1492086894)                   | Neal Ford et al. | On making difficult architectural decisions that most books gloss over. [Podcast (Tamil)](https://open.spotify.com/show/1RKGBC9lFaRIQhheGGNOeG?si=c0af72c5f7794fea) |
| [Enterprise Integration Patterns](https://www.amazon.com/Enterprise-Integration-Patterns-Designing-Deploying/dp/0321200683)                 | Hohpe & Woolf    | The catalog for messaging systems — channels, routing, transformation, endpoints.                                                                                   |
| [System Design Interview – Vol. 1](https://www.amazon.com/System-Design-Interview-insiders-Second/dp/B08CMF2CQF)                            | Alex Xu          | Practical walkthroughs of common system design interview problems.                                                                                                  |
| [System Design Interview – Vol. 2](https://www.amazon.com/System-Design-Interview-Insiders-Guide/dp/1736049119)                             | Alex Xu          | Deeper dives: Google Maps, Airbnb, YouTube, payment systems.                                                                                                        |
| [A Philosophy of Software Design](https://www.amazon.com/Philosophy-Software-Design-2nd/dp/173210220X)                                      | John Ousterhout  | Changed how I think about complexity.                                                                                                                               |
| [Domain-Driven Design](https://www.amazon.com/Domain-Driven-Design-Tackling-Complexity-Software/dp/0321125215)                              | Eric Evans       | The source on modeling complex domains. Dense but foundational.                                                                                                     |

## Communication & Leadership

| Book                                                                                                                       | Author                 | Notes                                                                                                    |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Radical Candor](https://www.amazon.com/Radical-Candor-Revised-Kick-Ass-Humanity/dp/1250235375)                            | Kim Scott              | Care personally, challenge directly. The framework for giving honest feedback without being harsh.       |
| [Difficult Conversations](https://www.amazon.com/Difficult-Conversations-Discuss-What-Matters/dp/0143118447)               | Stone, Patton & Heen   | How to navigate high-stakes conversations without blowups or avoidance.                                  |
| [Present! A Techie's Guide to Public Speaking](https://www.amazon.com/Present-Techies-Guide-Public-Speaking/dp/0996349308) | Vijayashanker & Catlin | Written for engineers. Practical, no fluff.                                                              |
| [Talk Like TED](https://www.amazon.com/Talk-Like-TED-Public-Speaking-Secrets/dp/1250041120)                                | Carmine Gallo          | Breaks down what makes TED talks compelling. Good structure and delivery models.                         |
| [The Art of Persuasion](https://www.amazon.com/Art-Persuasion-Winning-Without-Intimidation/dp/1933596139)                  | Bob Burg               | Winning without intimidation — useful for sales, negotiation, and everyday influence.                    |
| [The Personal MBA](https://www.amazon.com/Personal-MBA-Master-Art-Business/dp/1591845572)                                  | Josh Kaufman           | A dense overview of core business concepts. Good for engineers who want to understand the business side. |
